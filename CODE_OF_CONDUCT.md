# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, specific, and evidence-led. Give creators clear attribution, separate observation from fact, and discuss disagreements without harassment or personal attacks

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, doxxing, deliberate misattribution, fabricated evidence, spam, and repeated bad-faith promotion are not accepted

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit or remove content, comments, issues, or contributions that violate this standard. Report concerns through the private channel described in `SECURITY.md` when public discussion would expose sensitive information
